Lifting-sliding structures

Sliding aluminium doors are an ideal solution for glazing of terrace exits, balconies, winter gardens, and swimming pools. When closed, the sliding structures look like a large glass wall, which without any effort can be pushed aside or folded altogether, providing a wide and spacious exit.

General information:

  • visually appealing design;
  • space saving;
  • structures of various opening types,
  • easy-to-use and space saving,
  • creating maximum comfort;
  • broad color palette;
  • easy, almost noiseless move;
  • high level of thermal insulation;
  • burglary protection.

We offers you the following options of sliding systems:

  • parallel retractable sliding structures (PASK, Schüco, and Reynaers);
  • lift and slide systems (Schüco and Reynaers);
  • panoramic sliding systems (Schüco and Reynaers);
  • folding structures (Schüco and Reynaers).
